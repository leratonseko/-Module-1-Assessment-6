burglars

Spanish burglar bars are fitted on the exterior of the home and create a cage-like effect over the window allowing you to still open your window to the outside. This burglar bar offers a vertical style finish with horizontal cross bars at set places. Snap-off bolts are used to secure the Spanish Bar onto the wall.
